Coronavirus Action

Would you be able to help in the global effort against the new Coronavirus, from the safety of your own home? You can help give as many people as possible the opportunity to see our lesson about Covid-19 simply by: • Researching organisations who might be able to use or share the lesson • Making a list of the organisations you find • Passing the list on to us
